Knowledge Base

Everything you need to know about Necho.

Bridging communication is the act of facilitating a meaningful exchange between people who do not share the same language or medium of expression. At Necho, we bridge communication by using AI to translate complex sign language into spoken text instantly.
Necho bridges silence by giving users a real-time voice. Using a simple camera feed, our AI recognizes ASL and regional sign dialects, converting them to audio so hearing individuals can participate in the conversation without needing a human interpreter.
Silence is a fundamental part of the sign language experience. By "Bridging Silence," Necho doesn't replace the beauty of sign language; it connects that world to the hearing world, ensuring that silence is a choice, not a barrier.
Modern technology bridges barriers through advanced machine learning. Necho specifically utilizes computer vision and neural networks to process thousands of gesture variations per second, ensuring high accuracy and local dialect recognition.
